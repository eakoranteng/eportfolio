Secure Software Development

Concepts and application of secure software development and SDLC models.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify & manage security risks as part of a software development project.
  • Critically analyse development problems and determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to solve them.
  • Design, develop and adapt programs and to produce a solution that meets the design brief and critically evaluate solutions that are produced.
  • Systematically develop and implement the skills required to be an effective member of a development team in a virtual professional environment, adopting real-life perspectives on team roles and organisation.

Reflection

The Secure Software Development module was a valuable learning experience that helped me understand the critical aspects of developing secure applications.

Collaborative discussions enabled me to emphasise the importance of effective communication and finding a balance between visual representation and explanatory text. Our team project was an excellent opportunity to identify areas of strength and areas that required improvement in terms of effective teamwork. During the practical implementation phase, I learned valuable lessons on prioritising security features and aligning development efforts with assessment criteria.

Overall, this module deepened my understanding of secure software practices and shed light on the intricate balance required in the development process, providing me with a solid foundation for my future endeavours in secure software development.
